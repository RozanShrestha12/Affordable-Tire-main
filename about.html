About Us

Family and Locally Owned Since 1970s

Affordable Tire is a family and locally owned business specializing in Tire repairs. We've been serving the community for over 50 years.

Our Vision

To guide and educate car owners on optimal tire care, empowering them with knowledge and practices to ensure longevity and peak performance throughout their vehicle's lifespan.

Our Mission

"To deliver top-notch tire services with convenience, aiming to ease fear, anxiety, and confusion. Leveraging advancements in artificial intelligence and internet marketing, we strive to communicate and respond to our clients in real-time, ensuring a seamless experience at every step of their tire service journey."

Large Inventory of Quality Tires

As a family-owned local business, our expertise lies exclusively in tires and wheels. Our commitment to delivering the utmost value and service is unwavering. With 100% dedication to tires and wheels, we ensure the lowest prices and guarantee personalized assistance from our tire specialists every time you visit.
