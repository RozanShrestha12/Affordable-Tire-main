  • Lubricate moving components regularly: Check and lubricate driveline components such as CV and u-joints to prevent breakdowns.
  • Check transmission and differential fluid: Look for signs like unusual noises or sluggish shifting, and consult your owner’s manual for fluid specifications.

Car Care Tips

Follow the car care tips below to keep your vehicle running reliably:

  • Regular Maintenance: Keep up with scheduled maintenance like oil changes, fluid checks, and tire rotations.
  • Check Tire Pressure: Maintain proper tire pressure to improve fuel efficiency and extend tire life.
  • Keep It Clean: Regularly wash and wax your car to protect the paint and keep it looking new.
  • Inspect Lights: Check headlights, taillights, and turn signals regularly to ensure they're working properly.
  • Monitor Fluid Levels: Check oil, coolant, brake fluid, and windshield washer fluid levels regularly and top up as needed.
  • Replace Wiper Blades: Replace worn wiper blades to ensure clear visibility during rainy or snowy weather.
  • Inspect Belts and Hoses: Check belts and hoses for signs of wear or damage and replace them if necessary.
  • Keep Battery Terminals Clean: Clean battery terminals to prevent corrosion and ensure a reliable electrical system.
  • Maintain Proper Alignment: Regularly check and adjust wheel alignment to prevent uneven tire wear and improve handling.
Following the tips and service schedule in your owner’s manual helps prevent big repairs and unexpected breakdowns. Regular maintenance not only saves money but also keeps you and your passengers safe. Neglecting suggested services can void warranties, so it's important to stay on top of maintenance.
